    20+ years experience in music strategy, supervision & licensing

    Storytelling is my passion. Music is my method. I’ve built a career by elevating what you see with what you hear.

    I also consult on corporate brand strategy and have advised clients such as Apple, JBL, Zynga, and Samsung on the most effective ways to integrate music with advertising/digital media in order to maximize consumer outreach.
